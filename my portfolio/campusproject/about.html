About Campus Lost & Found

Campus Lost & Found is a platform designed to help students easily report and recover lost items within the college campus. Students often lose important belongings such as ID cards, books, wallets, and electronic devices. Our website provides a simple system where users can report lost items and upload found items.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make the campus more helpful by allowing students to quickly return lost items to their owners.

Features

  • Report Lost Items
  • Report Found Items
  • Search Lost Items
  • Easy communication between students
